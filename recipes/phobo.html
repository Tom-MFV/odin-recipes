Pho Bo

Ingredients

  • Beef bones: 1.5 kg
  • Beef brisket: 500 g
  • Rice noodles (bánh phở): 400 g
  • Onion: 1 whole
  • Ginger: 50 g
  • Star anise: 4 pieces
  • Cloves: 5 pieces
  • Cinnamon stick: 1 stick
  • Fish sauce: 4 tbsp
  • Salt: 1 tbsp

Steps

  1. Boil beef in water, then remove and clean.
  2. Add onion, ginger, and spices to pot with water.
  3. Cook beef in broth until soft.
  4. Boil noodles, place in bowl, add beef and soup.
